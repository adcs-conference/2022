Attending ADCS

Here you can find useful information about attending ADCS 2022. You may also wish to refer to the ALTA website for further information.

Location

ADCS 2022 will be co-located with ALTA at Flinders University in Adelaide, Australia. The ALTA tutorials will be held on the 14th of December at Flinders' Victoria Square campus, which is located in the heart of Adelaide's CBD.
ADCS will be held at Flinders' Tonsley campus, located in Tonsley. The Tonsley campus is just 10 km from Adelaide’s CBD with a direct rail link and bus services available.

Food and Drink

There are many food and drink venues of various kinds around both the City and Tonsley sites as well as in the Glenelg precint a tramride away (where the conference dinner will be held). Morning and afternoon tea will be provided on site for registrants.

Accommodation

Please see the list of venues on the ALTA website for further information.

Visas and Invitation Letters

Visas and entry requirements differ country-to-country. For the latest information, refer to the Australian Government guidelines.
Any attendee requiring an invitation letter is urged to contact the chairs at the following email address: adcs2022@easychair.org.
