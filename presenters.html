Information for Presenters


All papers will be presented through a live call, with an allocated time limit given to each paper. The length of time allocated will depend on the length of the paper:

  • Papers with 6 pages (or less) are allocated 20 minutes.
  • Papers with 7 pages (or more) are allocated 25 minutes.

Although the time can be used at the presenter's discretion, we urge the presenter to leave at least 5 minutes for questions. It's worth keeping in mind that the seminar format allows attendees to access and read the paper before the presentation, so allowing for lively discussion is encouraged.
