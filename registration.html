Format

ADCS 2021 will run as an online seminar series. There will be weekly talks scheduled from December 2021 through to February 2022, with a few breaks (for Christmas and the holiday period). The seminars will be hosted on Zoom, and links will be circulated to all registrants. Please see the programme for more information.
